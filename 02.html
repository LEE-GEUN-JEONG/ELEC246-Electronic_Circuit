♣ Problem 8.19 (Ans : Solution 참고)

(a) Vg1 = Vg2 = 0
VGS1 = VGS2 = VOV1,2 + Vin
Thus, Vs1 = Vs2 = -(Vov1,2 + Vin)

(b) VDS of Q3 is zero, so zero current flows in Q3, Q3 will have an overdrive voltage of
VOV3 = VC-VS1,2-Vin = VC+VOV1,2

(c) with VG1=vid/2 and VG2 -Vid/2 where vid is a small signal,a small signal will appear between drain and source of Q3.
transistor Q3 will be operating in the triode region and its drain-source resistance rDS will be given by rDS = 1/unCox(W/L)3VOV3
Rs = 1/unCox(W/L)3VOV3
gm1,2 = unCox(W/L)1,2Vov1,2
gm3=unCox(W/L)3Vov3
Thus, Rs=(1/gm1,2)*Vov1,2/Vov3

(d) i) Vov3=Vov1,2 ----> VC=0
ii) VOV3=2VOV1,2 ----> VC=VOV1,2
